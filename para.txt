A food waste truck is a specialized vehicle designed to collect, transport, and sometimes process organic food waste from households, restaurants, markets, or food processing units. These trucks play an essential role in sustainable waste management systems by diverting biodegradable waste from landfills and promoting composting or energy recovery.

Key Features:

Sealed compartments to prevent leakage and odor.

Partitioned storage for different types of organic waste.

Hydraulic systems for easy loading and unloading.

GPS tracking and route optimization for efficiency.

Some are equipped with on-board compactors or shredders.
